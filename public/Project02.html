Little Chef - Board Game

Fun way to learn about your food

Introduction

Little Chef is an educational board game designed for children aged 8 years and older, offering a fun learning experience about different foods and nutrition.

Project Context

Our business project involved developing an educational board/card game for kids. Our team of five conducted individual research on existing games and collaborated on brainstorming sessions. We decided to create a game that teaches children about foods and nutrition, aiming to educate in a fun way while promoting social skills and strategic thinking.
